A Hidden Gem in Lilli Pilli

Nestled on the coast, Three66 Espresso Bar offers stunning water views, a relaxed vibe, and a fully licensed bar. Whether you're dropping by for your 5am caffeine hit, grabbing a famous bacon and egg roll, or enjoying a leisurely lunch on our deck, we have something for everyone.

We are proudly pet-friendly and packed with lush indoor plants to make you feel right at home.
