The Vibe

A Hidden Gem on the Bay

At Three66, we blend premium café culture with the relaxed beauty of the coast. We proudly serve specialty ONA Coffee, ensuring every cup is poured to absolute perfection.

Whether you are dropping by for your 5 AM caffeine hit, grabbing our famous bacon and egg roll, or enjoying a leisurely lunch on the deck, we have something for everyone. We are proudly pet-friendly, fully licensed, and packed with lush indoor plants to make you feel right at home.
